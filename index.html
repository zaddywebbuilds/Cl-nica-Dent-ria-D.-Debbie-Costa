Elevating Care.

We are a family-run dental practice in Cedros, at the heart of Faial Island. For years, we have cared for patients across the island — from Horta to Flamengos — building relationships that span generations.

We are not a corporate chain. We are your local dentist: the one who remembers your last visit, asks about your family, and takes the time to explain your treatment properly.
